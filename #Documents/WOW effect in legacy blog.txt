 add the fade-in effect elsewhere, just add these classes to any element:

html
<div class="wow fadeInUp" data-wow-delay=".2s">
    Your content here
</div>
Available animation classes:

fadeInUp - slides up while fading in

fadeInDown - slides down while fading in

fadeInLeft - slides from left while fading in

fadeInRight - slides from right while fading in

fadeIn - simple fade in

bounceIn - bounce effect

zoomIn - zoom in effect

Delay options:

data-wow-delay=".1s" - 0.1 second delay

data-wow-delay=".3s" - 0.3 second delay

data-wow-delay=".5s" - 0.5 second delay

Example usage:

html
<h3 class="wow fadeInLeft" data-wow-delay=".3s">Section Title</h3>
<p class="wow fadeInRight" data-wow-delay=".5s">Section content</p>
<button class="wow bounceIn" data-wow-delay=".7s">Click me</button>
